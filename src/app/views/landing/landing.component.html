Obstetrics and Gynecology

Data collection of cyclic data on pregnant women.

The Project

This project is initiated by Dr. Nana Kobi. The purpose is to collect data of pregnant women to aid research.

Maternity Records

When you’re giving birth, you’re probably not at your most observant. So it’s sometimes interesting to check out your maternity notes and get a different perspective on your birth experience.

For Data Analysis

The purpose of collecting this data is to extract useful information from data and taking the decision based upon the data analysis.
